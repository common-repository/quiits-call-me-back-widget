= How does Call me back widget work? =

* Sign Up for a Free Trial to establish an account in [callmebackwidget.com](https://www.callmebackwidget.com/). 
* Upload this plugin to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
* Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
* Site visitors can click on the widget icon to contact you directly.
* Customer clicks on the callmeback widget and enters his phone number. 
* The script automatically calls you and the customer simultaneously.
* Callmeback unites 2 outgoing calls into a single conversation.
* The conversation is recorded, and you receive a report via e-mail.

== Installation ==
 
1.  Create a Free account in [callmebackwidget.com](https://www.callmebackwidget.com/)
2.  Upload your plugin folder to the '/wp-content/plugins' directory.
3.  Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
4.  Delete Cache and Minified CSS/JS, If CallMeBackWidget button doesn't show up on your website. [How to Clear Your Cache in WordPress](https://www.wpbeginner.com/beginners-guide/how-to-clear-your-cache-in-wordpress/).
